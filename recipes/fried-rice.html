Take-out style Vegetarian Fried Rice

Do you love take-out fried rice but don't know how to make it homemade? Well this is the perfect recipe for Take-out styled fried rice!

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons canola oil, divided
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 carrots. chopped
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 scallions, minced
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
