D's Famous Salsa

Fresh homemade salsa and you probably already have the ingredients at home already! Quick, easy, and delicious salsa!

Ingredients

  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, finely diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
